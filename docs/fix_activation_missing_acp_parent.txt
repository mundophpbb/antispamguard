Fix: activation after Delete data could fail with:
Um modulo requerido nao existe: ACP_ANTISPAMGUARD_TITLE

Cause:
The consolidated migration tried to add ACP child modes under ACP_ANTISPAMGUARD_TITLE
without recreating the parent ACP category first. After phpBB Delete data, that parent
category is removed from the modules table.

Change:
v_3_3_15 now creates the parent category under ACP_CAT_DOT_MODS before adding the
settings/logs/stats/about modes. v_3_3_16 then adds the StopForumSpam mode and
v_3_3_17 reorders the menu.
